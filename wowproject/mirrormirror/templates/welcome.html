Our early childhood experiences can affect our health and wellbeing as adults.
In fact, traumatic childhood experiences are surprisingly common;
roughly two-thirds of us have lived through at least one.



The 10 Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs):

  • Sexual Abuse
  • Domestic Violence
  • Physical Abuse
  • Emotional Abuse
  • Physical Neglect
  • Emotional Neglect
  • Parent of caregiver addicted to drugs or alcohol
  • Parent or caregiver who is depressed or mentally ill
  • Household member/s has been incarcerated
  • Parents divorced or separated

Taking the ACE quiz is a pivotal starting point in one's healing journey.
